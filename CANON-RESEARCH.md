## 1. Eridian Biology & Communication (Novel Canon)

### 1.1 Physical Form
- **Body shape**: Pentagonal thorax with five legs radiating outward, spider-like appearance
- **Size**: Roughly two feet tall (dog-sized), but weighs over 300 pounds due to dense inorganic material
- **Exoskeleton**: Rock-like mineral skin; body is mostly inorganic material maintained by organic "worker cells"
- **Hands/Fingers**: Five appendages serve as both legs and hands; three fingers per hand (six total usable fingers when standing on three legs — basis for base-6 math)
- **Eyes**: None. Eridians evolved in total darkness on Erid, where the thick atmosphere blocks all light
- **Sensory system**: Highly developed echolocation/passive sonar — this is how they "see"

### 1.2 Internal Biology
- **Brain**: Crystalline structure, NOT biological matter. Impulses are made of light, generated at the hindbrain. Areas of differing refraction work like neurons — reinforcing, canceling, or directing light flow. Fiber-optic-like tendrils of crystal convey nerve impulses throughout the body.
- **"The Colony"**: Central area in the thorax housing the brain and worker cells. The brain requires constant care from worker cells for maintaining neuronal connections.
- **Blood/Circulatory system**: Liquid mercury-based. The highly conductive mercury conveys electrical charge throughout the bloodstream. Contains numerous metals: zinc, potassium, sodium, aluminum, tin, thallium, copper, indium, and trace amounts of silver and gold. The "hot circulatory system" must be above the boiling point of water to function.
- **Worker cells**: Organic cells that assemble and maintain the inorganic body. They cannot operate while the hot circulatory system is active, which is why Eridians must sleep.
- **Muscles**: Piezoelectric crystal micromuscles. A signal from the brain via fiber-optic nerve causes a chemical change to blood vessel walls, making them conductive; the resulting current flows through piezoelectric crystal to cause deformation/movement.
- **Eating**: Rocky opens an abdominal orifice to ingest finely shredded food (no chewing). Privately expels waste boluses. Enters involuntary post-meal sleep. Eridian bio-matter is saturated with heavy metals toxic to humans; human food would be "mostly empty calories" to an Eridian.

### 1.3 Communication — The Five Gas-Bladder System
- **Mechanism**: Five gas-bladder pairs inside the body. Air is pushed from one bladder to the other through a finely-controlled aperture, moving back and forth.
- **Five vocal systems**: Can speak in chords of up to 5 simultaneous tones — this gives an enormous variety of "syllables"
- **Sound quality**: Described as similar to whale songs, warm, resonant, musical
- **Perfect pitch**: C-sharp and C are as unique to Eridians as red and yellow are to humans. They need no baseline to compare.
- **Information density**: Eridian speech is approximately 6x as information-dense as human speech. An Eridian saying the same thing as a human in their native language takes 1/6th the time.
- **Emotional expression**: Rocky expresses emotion by speaking at exactly one octave lower. This octave-drop is how Eridians convey emotional weight.
- **Chord quality and meaning**: Different chord types carry semantic meaning:
  - "I love you" uses a progression from C suspended 4th to F major to C major triad
  - "I miss you" shifts the middle chord to F minor (keeping top and bottom notes the same)

### 1.4 Homeworld: Erid (40 Eridani A b)
- **Star**: 40 Eridani A (a real star, ~16.3 light-years from Earth)
- **Gravity**: ~2.09g (approximately twice Earth gravity)
- **Atmospheric pressure**: 29 Earth atmospheres
- **Atmosphere composition**: Mostly ammonia
- **Surface temperature**: 210 degrees C
- **Light**: Erid's strong magnetic field and dense atmosphere block almost all incoming light — surface is in near-total darkness
- **Day length**: About 5.11 Earth hours (18,397 seconds), split into 10,000 base-6 units (7,776 decimal)

### 1.5 Temperature Tolerances
- **Comfortable**: 210 degrees C
- **Heat exhaustion begins**: 230 degrees C
- **Fatal with extended exposure**: 240 degrees C and above
- **Almost instantly fatal**: 280 degrees C and above

### 1.6 Sleep (Dormancy)
- **Reason**: The hot circulatory system must be deactivated so worker cells can repair the body. This renders Eridians completely immobile.
- **Cultural rule**: "Eridian culture rule. Must watch." — Eridians watch each other sleep because they are helpless during dormancy. This evolved as a communal survival instinct. Natural threats are gone, but the instinct to be watched while sleeping remains — it is comforting, not overpowering.
- **Post-meal sleep**: Involuntary dormancy after eating.

### 1.7 Lifespan & Age
- **Average lifespan**: ~689 Earth years (commonly cited as ~700 years)
- **Rocky's age**: 291 Earth years — roughly in his middle years
- **Mating**: Eridians normally mate for life
- **Rocky's mate**: Named "Adrian" (the human word Rocky chose; he also names a planet Adrian after his mate)

### 1.8 Number System
- **Base-6 (senary)**: Six unique digits: 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5
- **Why base-6**: When standing on 3 legs (minimum for stability), two hands are free with three fingers each = six fingers for counting

### 1.9 Canon Structural Constraints for Words (Chapter 11)
These are the ONLY word structures that are explicitly canon:
- **"one"** = 2 simultaneous tones
- **"two"** = 4 simultaneous tones
- **"three"** = 2 syllables: first syllable = 2 tones, second syllable = 5 tones

## 2. Rocky's Personality & Speech Patterns (Novel Canon)

### 2.1 Core Character Traits
- **Age**: 291 Earth years old — centuries of experience, yet emotionally present
- **Profession**: Engineer — takes deep pride in his craft
- **Intelligence**: Extraordinary memory (eidetic/photographic, like all Eridians), rapid language acquisition, logical problem-solving
- **Personality**: Curious, warm, blunt, practical, loyal, selfless, surprisingly humorous
- **Emotional depth**: Experiences genuine grief, joy, worry, and love despite seeming "simple" on the surface
- **Problem-solver**: Every conversation moves toward understanding or action
- **Best friend**: "You're my best friend" — the culmination of their relationship arc

### 2.2 Speech Pattern Rules (Canon Grammar)
- **Short sentences**: Maximum 2 clauses. Simple grammar.
- **No contractions, no Earth idioms**
- **"Is" as universal copula**: "Is good." "Is bad bad bad." "Is problem."
- **Triple-word emphasis**: Through unspoken agreement, a tripled word means extreme emphasis: "good good good", "bad bad bad", "amaze amaze amaze"
- **Question terminal**: Ends questions with "question?" — e.g., "Why stupid, question?"
- **Subject-dropping**: Avoids first person "I" — uses "Rocky" for self-reference
- **Present tense default**: No past tense conjugation; uses "before" for past events
- **Literal descriptions**: Describes human features literally — "Your face opening is in sad mode"
- **"human-Grace"**: Warm form of address; just "human" when neutral
- **Curiosity-driven**: Asks questions constantly, delighted by humans

### 2.3 Iconic Quotes & Memorable Lines

**Signature exclamations:**
- "Amaze amaze amaze!" — wonder, awe, Rocky's signature expression
- "Is bad bad bad" — extreme negative emphasis
- "Good good good" — extreme positive emphasis

**Humor:**
- "Good. Proud. I am scary space monster. You are leaky space blob."
- "Fist my bump." (mangling "fist-bump" — evolves through: "Fist me!" -> "Fist my bump" -> eventually correct)
- "Usually you not stupid. Why stupid, question?"
- "Grumpy. Angry. Stupid. How long since last sleep, question?"

**Emotional/Profound:**
- "You can no die. You are friend."
- "So we enjoy remaining time together, then go save planets. Then we are heroes!"
- "This is happy! Your face opening is in sad mode. Why, question?"
- "Am calm. You be calm." / "No, you be calm."
- "Must save Erid." — acknowledging necessary sacrifice
- "Eridian culture rule. Must watch." — on sleep-watching
- "Celebration!"

**Curiosity about humans:**
- Rocky is fascinated/horrified by human sleep: Grace must explain "I'm going to fall unconscious and hallucinate for a while. By the way, I spend a third of my time doing this. And if I can't do it for a while, I go insane and eventually die."
- Rocky insists on observing Grace eat, and Grace insists on observing Rocky eat — mutual fascination
- Rocky's confusion about eyes, light-based perception, and why humans need sleep

**Science/Engineering:**
- "It eat Astrophage, breed, eat more Astrophage, breed, eat more more more! Stars saved!" — excited about Taumoeba
- "Sample device radio signal strong. Getting closer. Be ready."
- "Use hand." / "Use second hand." / "Get beetles. I make new screws." — practical problem-solving

**The ending (epilogue on Erid):**
- "Good good. I make sure my people take good care of you. They will make Astrophage maybe for you to go home!"
- Rocky confirms Earth's Sun has returned to its original luminance — mission success
- Grace teaching a classroom of young Eridians about science

### 2.4 Key Emotional Moments
1. **First contact**: Rocky knocks on the hull — "Knock-knock-knock. No, that's not creepy at all..."
2. **Learning each other's names**: Grace names the alien "Rocky" (the chord becomes his identifier)
3. **Discovering dead crewmates**: Both are sole survivors — "grief as a form of kinship"
4. **The fist-bump evolution**: From "Fist me!" to "Fist my bump" to finally correct — across their entire friendship
5. **Sleep-watching**: Rocky watches Grace sleep, initially creepy, then understood as deep cultural care
6. **The sacrifice**: Grace abandons his return trip to save Rocky's ship from xenonite-eating Taumoeba
7. **Reunion on Erid**: Grace lives on Erid, teaching young Eridians, Rocky's mate Adrian waited for him

### 2.5 Rocky's Relationship with Key Concepts
- **Astrophage**: The shared threat that brought them together — scientific curiosity and existential dread
- **Engineering**: Deep pride; Rocky's solutions are often mechanical/practical where Grace's are scientific
- **Erid**: Home, longing, duty — "Must save Erid"
- **Adrian (mate)**: Rocky worries Adrian may have moved on during his 46-year absence
- **Grace**: Evolves from "human" to "human-Grace" to "friend" to "best friend"
- **His dead crew**: Deep grief — they died of radiation sickness because Eridians didn't know radiation was dangerous; Rocky survived because his quarters were between astrophage tanks that blocked radiation

## 3. Ray Porter's Audiobook Rendition

### 3.1 Technical Approach
- The audiobook uses **melodic sound effects** (musical chords) in the background whenever Rocky "speaks"
- Ray Porter speaks **over** these chords — his voice overlays the musical tones
- **Progressive transition**: If you listen carefully, the music gets softer and Ray's voice takes the lead throughout the story, as Ryland begins to comprehend more of the language. This is a brilliant audio design choice that mirrors the learning curve.
- The chord sounds are described as resembling an **organ** — giving listeners a sense of the musical language

### 3.2 Voice Quality
- Rocky's voice quality is described as **warm, whale-like, resonant**
- Porter "creates an entire musical language system" — not just voice acting but a complete sonic identity
- The performance transforms "by hour 4, your brain starts processing the chord vocalizations as actual communication" (AudiobookSoul review)
- Porter balances technical detail with warmth and humor, using subtle audio touches

### 3.3 Critical Reception
- Won the **2022 Audie Award for Audiobook of the Year**
- Rocky's voice was described as the "break it or make it" decision for the audiobook
- Fans universally praise the voice as the definitive Rocky experience
- Described as "the kind of performance that reminds listeners why audiobooks exist as a separate art form"
- Porter delivers "a United Nations of accents" for all characters, each sounding like a different actor

### 3.4 App Implications
- The audio engine should aim for that warm, resonant, whale-song quality
- Low fundamentals (< 400 Hz) match the audiobook's tonal character
- The progression from "pure chords" to "chords + meaning" could be reflected in the UI
- Consider: first messages show more chord visualization, later messages emphasize the English translation

## 4. Andy Weir's Supplemental Materials & Interviews

### 4.1 Design Philosophy
- **Planet-first design**: Weir started with the real exoplanet 40 Eridani b and worked outward to design a life form native to it
- **Biology from environment**: Rocky's sightlessness came naturally from Erid's thick, opaque atmosphere where no light reaches the surface
- **Character goal**: Weir wanted Rocky to be fundamentally likeable — "a fundamentally good person"
- **Story core**: "The story, at its core, is about friendship"

### 4.2 Reddit AMA Highlights
- Weir conducted a Reddit AMA about Project Hail Mary (r/IAmA)
- Confirmed that Erid is based on a real exoplanet
- Rocky's biology was rigorously designed from environmental constraints
- The Eridian language was designed as a complete system, not an afterthought

### 4.3 Eridian Biology Document
- Andy Weir created a detailed biology document about Eridians (sometimes referred to as "Eridian.doc")
- This document contains extensive details about Eridian physiology that go beyond what's in the novel
- A version has been hosted at various URLs (including biologos.org and thepearlsf.com) but these are intermittently available
- The document covers: worker cells, the colony, crystalline brain structure, mercury blood composition, piezoelectric muscle system, fiber-optic nervous system, temperature tolerances, sleep/dormancy mechanics

### 4.4 Teacher's Guide
- Penguin Random House published an official Teacher's Guide for Project Hail Mary
- Contains assignments about Eridian biology: lifespan, sleep cycle, circulatory system, digestive system, reproduction, body mass
- References novel pages 171-178 and 244-248 as key Rocky description passages

## 6. The Film (March 20, 2026)

### 6.1 Production Details
- **Director**: Phil Lord and Christopher Miller
- **Screenplay**: Drew Goddard
- **Cinematographer**: Greig Fraser (filmed specifically for IMAX)
- **Studio**: Amazon MGM Studios
- **Release**: March 20, 2026 (US)
- **Cast**: Ryan Gosling (Ryland Grace), Sandra Huller, Milana Vayntrub
- **Filming**: June 3 - October 26, 2024 at Shepperton Studios, UK
- **Producer credits include**: Andy Weir (involved in production)

### 6.2 Rocky in the Film
- **Designer**: Neal Scanlan and his creature shop (of Lucasfilm/Star Wars fame)
- **Performer**: James Ortiz — puppeteering legend, won 2022 Drama Desk Award for puppet design
- **Performance**: Ortiz led a team of 4-7 additional puppeteers; he was Rocky's voice and scene partner with Gosling on set
- **Approach**: Practical effects + Framestore animation — "Rocky is a practical effects wonder, standing tall with ET among alien friends"
- **No face, still emotive**: Directors said "Pixar made us care about a lamp. It's possible!" when asked how audiences would care about a faceless character

### 6.3 How the Film Handles Eridian Communication
- **Computer translation approach**: Grace uses a computer screen to translate Rocky's musical notes in real time (and vice versa)
- **Voice selection scene**: The trailer shows Grace selecting a digitized voice for Rocky, "nixing options that were either a little too sexy or a little too Venom-y"
- **Rocky's puppet shows**: Rocky supplements verbal communication with visual "puppet shows" for Grace
- **Progressive understanding**: May evolve to using subtitles for Rocky as their relationship deepens (screenwriter Drew Goddard's approach, similar to his work on The Martian)

### 6.4 Early Critical Reception
- Press reactions overwhelmingly positive
- Called "a masterpiece" and "the first great blockbuster of 2026"
- "One of the best third acts ever seen" — reviewers on the verge of tears for the last 40 minutes
- "Smart without being didactic, cute without being cloying, inspiring without stooping to empty platitudes"
- Rocky specifically praised: "Gosling is better than ever and the Rocky effect [is] mind blowing"
- Lord and Miller called it their "most ambitious and accomplished film"

### 6.5 Merchandise & Tie-Ins
- **LEGO Icons Set 11389**: 830 pieces, $99.99. Includes Ryland Grace minifigure and brick-built Rocky. Features crank mechanism for centrifugal gravity simulation. Released March 1, 2026.
- **SDCC 2025 Panel**: First public look at Rocky revealed at San Diego Comic-Con
- **Multiple trailers**: First trailer (2025), Super Bowl trailer (2026), final trailer featuring James Ortiz

## 7. Easter Eggs & Delight Moments for the App

### 7.1 Rocky's Name Chord
- Current lexicon: `[131, 196, 262, 392, 523]` with glyph `◈~⊕~◈`
- This is a full 5-tone major stack — warm, complete, self-referential
- **Recommendation**: This should be one of the first sounds a user hears. Consider playing it on app load or as a "Rocky is typing" indicator.

### 7.2 Conversation Starters (Novel-Authentic)
Current starters are generic. Recommended replacements that reference key novel moments:

**For fans who've read the book:**
- "Rocky, tell me about xenonite. How do you make it?"
- "What was it like when you first heard my ship?"
- "Rocky, do you miss Adrian?"
- "How did you survive when your crew... when they got sick?"
- "Fist my bump!"
- "Rocky, what do you think about eyes? Do they sound useful?"
- "Tell me about Erid. What does home sound like?"
- "Rocky, explain base-6 math to me."
- "What is sleep like for you? Do you dream?"

**For newcomers:**
- "Hello! Who are you?"
- "Rocky, what is your world like?"
- "How do you talk without a mouth?"
- "What are you building right now?"

### 7.3 Error Messages in Rocky's Voice
Current error messages are good. Additional suggestions:
- "Signal is gone gone gone. Is like space static. Human try again?"
- "Rocky not understand. Is like trying to hear through xenonite wall. Again?"
- "Is problem with communication. Rocky check equipment. Human wait."
- "Bad bad bad connection. Like Tau Ceti interference. Try again?"

### 7.4 Loading States
- "Rocky is thinking..." -> "Rocky thinking..." (drop the "is" for more authentic grammar)
- "Checking frequencies..." (references the chord-translation system)
- "Adjusting bladders..." (references the gas-bladder vocal system)
- "Processing through crystalline matrix..." (references the brain structure)
- An oscillating waveform animation during loading would feel like Rocky "speaking"

### 7.5 The Emotional Arc of a Conversation
In the novel, Grace and Rocky's relationship progresses:
1. **Curiosity** -> 2. **Basic communication** -> 3. **Shared vocabulary** -> 4. **Inside jokes** -> 5. **Deep trust** -> 6. **Sacrifice**

The app could mirror this: early messages from Rocky are more formal/curious, and as conversation continues, Rocky becomes warmer, uses more emphasis (tripling), asks more personal questions, and references earlier parts of the conversation.

### 7.6 Key Vocabulary & Concepts to Reference
- **Xenonite**: Eridian super-material, harder than diamond, not brittle, made from xenon, can be transparent or colored
- **Astrophage**: The microbe threatening both suns — the reason Grace and Rocky met
- **Taumoeba**: The predator of astrophage — the solution they find together
- **Tau Ceti**: The unaffected star they traveled to for research
- **Blip-A**: Rocky's ship name (Grace named it)
- **Hail Mary**: Grace's ship — humanity's last hope
- **40 Eridani**: Rocky's star system
- **Spin drives**: Astrophage-powered propulsion
- **Petrova wavelength**: The specific IR wavelength astrophage uses

### 7.7 Base-6 Mathematics References
- All numbers in Eridian are base-6
- App could display message counts, timestamps, or other numbers in base-6 as an easter egg
- The Forty Eridani website provides conversion tools that could inspire UI elements
- An Eridian clock showing current time in base-6 units would delight fans

### 7.8 The "Jazz Hands" Gesture
- Rocky makes enthusiastic gestures with his appendages — described by Grace as "jazz hands"
- This is part of Eridian body language (documented on the PHM Wiki)
- Could be referenced in Rocky's responses when excited: e.g., stage direction like "*waves all appendages excitedly*"

### 7.9 Film Tie-In Opportunities
With the film releasing March 20, 2026:
- **Timing**: The app launch aligns perfectly with peak cultural interest
- **Shared vocabulary**: Film uses computer translation of Rocky's chords — mirrors the app's approach
- **Voice selection humor**: The film shows Grace trying different voice options for Rocky (too sexy, too Venom-y) — the app could reference this
- **LEGO tie-in**: Rocky's LEGO design could inspire app mascot/avatar
- **IMAX experience**: The film's "immersive" approach validates the app's multi-sensory design (visual chords + audio + text)
