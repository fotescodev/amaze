# Story Events — Canon Timeline

> Source: *Project Hail Mary* by Andy Weir (2021). Key plot events relevant to the Rocky/Grace relationship and the "Talk to Rocky" app.

---

## Act I: Discovery & First Contact

### Grace Wakes Up (Ch 1-9)
- Ryland Grace wakes from a coma alone on the Hail Mary, with amnesia
- Two crewmates (Yáo and Ilyukhina) died during the coma journey — never woke up
- Grace gradually remembers: he's a schoolteacher recruited by Eva Stratt to save Earth from Astrophage
- Astrophage is dimming the Sun — an extinction-level event
- The Hail Mary traveled 12 light-years to Tau Ceti to study the one unaffected star

### The Blip-A Arrives (Ch 10)
- Grace detects another ship in the Tau Ceti system — names it "Blip-A"
- Blip-A matches velocity and approaches
- A tunnel extends between the two ships — pressure-sealed connection

### First Contact (Ch 10)
- Grace enters the tunnel. Finds a hexagonal wall of different xenonite samples
- **"Knock, knock, knock."** — Rocky knocks from the other side
- Grace knocks back three times
- Through a clear hex, Grace sees Rocky's hand: three triangular fingers, rock-like skin
- Rocky holds up a tiny xenonite figurine of Grace's EVA suit + a model of the Hail Mary, gesturing "Go back to your ship"
- Grace gives thumbs-up. Rocky imitates with two fingers curled, one pointed up

> **Canon quote**: "Also, until I hear otherwise, his name is Rocky."

### Full Reveal (Ch 10)
- Rocky replaces the hex wall with clear xenonite. Grace enters the tunnel.
- "He's a spider. A big-assed spider." — Grace's first full sight
- Rocky waves. Grace waves back. Rocky waves again. Grace shakes head — no more waving.
- Rocky speaks: musical notes — Grace's jaw drops. Musical language.
- Rocky leaves gifts: beaded spheres showing atomic structures (hydrogen = 8 protons for Erid's star, oxygen = 7 for Tau Ceti's star)

## Act II: Building a Language

### The Clock (Ch 10-11)
- Rocky presents a mechanical clock with base-6 digits
- Grace learns: base-6 numbers, reading direction (left to right), Eridian time units
- One Eridian second = 2.366 Earth seconds

### Building Vocabulary (Ch 11-12)
- Numbers first, then length (tape measure through mini-airlock), then mass
- Rocky creates a small airlock ("squarelock") in the dividing wall for exchanging objects
- They exchange scientific concepts: gravity, temperature, atmosphere, compounds
- Grace writes translation software — waveform analyzer + dictionary spreadsheet
- Rocky memorizes instantly; Grace needs the laptop

### The Octave Drop (Ch 12)
- Rocky's translator outputs gibberish. Grace bumps the audio up one octave.
- Result: "Original crew was twenty-three. Now is only me."
- Grace realizes the octave drop = emotion/grief
- "Bad bad bad." — Both lost their crews. Shared grief becomes their bond.
- Grace puts his hand on the divider. Rocky puts a claw opposite. "I'll watch you sleep."

### Sleep-Watching Begins (Ch 12)
- Rocky insists on watching Grace sleep every night
- Grace sleeps in the tunnel. Rocky brings repair work to keep busy while watching.
- Grace eventually learns this is deep Eridian cultural need: "Eridian culture rule. Must watch."

## Act III: Collaboration

### Exploring Each Other's Ships (Ch 13-14)
- Rocky builds a geodesic ball + life-support system to survive in Grace's cold environment
- Enters Grace's ship via magnets rolling along a metal track
- "Good good good room!" — Rocky's voice a full octave higher exploring the lab
- Rocky is amazed by computers: "Machine that think. Eridians no have that."
- Grace learns Eridian biology in detail — "Eridians are steam-powered"

### Radiation Revelation (Ch 13)
- Rocky's 23 crewmates died from radiation sickness — Eridians didn't know radiation existed
- Rocky survived because his workshop was between Astrophage fuel tanks (natural radiation shielding)
- "I not science Eridian. Smart smart smart science Eridians died." — Rocky's survivors' guilt

### The Adrian Conversation (Ch 16)
- Rocky reveals he has a mate back on Erid
- Grace names the mate "Adrian"
- Rocky worries: "Mate possibly has new mate. I gone a long time." (46 Earth years)
- "Sad. But necessary. Must save Erid."

### Eating Scene (Ch 16)
- Grace persuades Rocky to let him watch him eat: "Science."
- Rocky's discomfort: "Is biological. Is gross." / "Social discomfort. No talk."
- The abdominal opening, waste expulsion, meat consumption — Grace watches it all
- Rocky immediately falls asleep after eating. Grace watches.

### Age Revelation (Ch 14)
- "How long do Eridians live?" / "Average is six hundred eighty-nine years."
- "How many years have you been alive?" / "Two hundred ninety-one years."
- "Rocky is older than the United States."

### Gravity Test (Ch 14)
- Grace activates the centrifuge for 1g gravity
- "Not much gravity." — Erid's gravity is 20.48 m/s² (2.09g)
- Rocky's 168 kg = ~800 lbs on Erid. "And that's his native environment."

## Act IV: Saving Two Worlds

### Planet Adrian (Ch 17-18)
- They orbit Planet Adrian in the Tau Ceti system to study Astrophage breeding grounds
- Grace discovers Taumoeba — a microbe that eats Astrophage
- This is the solution: seed Venus/Threeworld with Taumoeba to control Astrophage around Sol/40 Eridani

### Taumoeba Victory (Ch 25)
- After extensive testing, Taumoeba-82.5 works in both Venus and Threeworld conditions
- Rocky bounces off walls in excitement, claws clattering
- "Good good good!" / "Erid will live! Earth will live! Everyone live!"
- Rocky curls his claw into a ball: **"Fist me!"** — first attempt at fist-bump
- Grace: "It's 'fist-bump,' but yeah."

### The Celebration (Ch 25-26)
- Grace finds Ilyukhina's vodka stash ("Five 1-liter bags labeled vodka")
- Rocky wears special celebration clothing: formal garment with rough gems, braided cords, gloves
- "To Taumoeba-82.5! Savior of two worlds!"
- "Human and Eridian work together, save everyone!"

### The Friendship Declaration (Ch 26)
- "You will miss me, question? I will miss you. You are friend."
- "You're my friend. Heck, you're my best friend. And pretty soon we're going to say goodbye forever."
- "So we enjoy remaining time together, then go save planets. Then we are heroes!"
- Rocky raises a wrench: "To us!" / Grace raises vodka: "To ush!" (slurring from vodka)

## Act V: Crisis & Sacrifice

### Taumoeba Eats Xenonite (Ch 26-27)
- Discovery: Taumoeba can eat through xenonite — Rocky's ship hull is xenonite
- Rocky's ship is compromised. His fuel bays are being consumed.
- If Rocky can't get home, Erid dies.

### Grace's Decision (Ch 27-28)
- Grace has already sent beetles (mini-probes) toward Earth with Taumoeba samples
- He could go home. Instead, he turns the Hail Mary around to chase Rocky's failing ship.
- He will never have enough food to survive the return trip to Earth.
- This is a one-way decision: save Rocky and Erid, never see Earth again.

### The Reunion (Ch 28)
- Grace catches Rocky's ship, transfers aboard
- Rocky trembles: **"No. You no can die. You are friend."**
- Grace: "It's okay. I made my decision. This is the only way to save both of our worlds."
- Rocky suggests Taumoeba as food for Grace — it has DNA, glucose, mitochondria, no heavy metals
- **"Fist my bump."** — Rocky's evolved attempt. Grace laughs: "It's just 'fist-bump.'"

## Epilogue: Life on Erid (Ch 29-30)

### Grace on Erid (16 years later)
- Grace lives in a dome on Erid, maintained by 30 Eridians
- Ate Taumoeba to survive, then "meburgers" (lab-grown clones of his own muscle tissue)
- Uses a cane — aging + high gravity
- Eridians made him a wristwatch, dome with lights on 24-hour cycle

### The Thrum
- Eridians have a collective discussion mechanism: "thrum" — everyone talks at once, the group arrives at conclusions smarter than any individual
- "In a way, Eridians can become ad-hoc neurons in a group mind."
- Second-largest science thrum ever held to figure out how to keep Grace alive

### Sol Is Saved
- Rocky delivers the news: "Your star has returned to full luminance!"
- Grace weeps. "Is this a fist-bump situation?" / "This is a monumentally epic fist-bump situation."
- "We won. Simple as that."

### Teaching
- Grace teaches a class of 30 young Eridians using an Earthlike organ keyboard
- Speaks fluent Eridian via keyboard with inflection, tone, mood controls
- "All right, all right. Everyone settle down and get in your seats."
- "Who here can tell me the speed of light?" — Twelve kids raise their claws.

### Final Exchange
- Rocky: "Go do your job, old man."
- Grace: "Later, Rocky." / Rocky: "Later!"
- Rocky goes home to watch Adrian sleep.
