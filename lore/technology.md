# Technology — Canon Reference

> Source: *Project Hail Mary* by Andy Weir (2021). Key technology and science concepts.

---

## 1. Astrophage

### What It Is
- A single-celled organism that absorbs and stores energy from stars
- Migrates between stars to breed, consuming stellar energy in transit
- Stores energy at exactly 96 degrees Celsius (the Petrova temperature)
- Causes stars to dim — an extinction-level threat to planets orbiting affected stars
- Discovered by Dr. Petrova (hence "Petrova wavelength" — the IR wavelength it uses)

### Properties
- Stores an incredible amount of energy per unit mass
- Absorbs all radiation (perfect blackbody absorber) — blocks everything including neutrinos
- This property makes it the perfect rocket fuel AND radiation shielding
- Both the Hail Mary and the Blip-A use Astrophage as propellant for their spin drives
- Rocky's workshop was shielded from radiation by surrounding Astrophage fuel tanks — this saved his life

### The Threat
- Astrophage populations around Sol and 40 Eridani were dimming both stars
- Earth faced an ice age and eventual extinction
- Erid faced the same — cooling would kill the entire biosphere
- Tau Ceti was the one nearby star NOT affected — both missions went there to find out why

## 2. Taumoeba

### What It Is
- An amoeba native to Planet Adrian (in the Tau Ceti system)
- A natural predator of Astrophage — eats Astrophage and breeds rapidly
- The reason Tau Ceti's star was unaffected: Taumoeba controlled the Astrophage population

### Properties
- Has DNA, mitochondria, uses glucose — standard Earth-compatible biochemistry
- Does NOT contain heavy metals (unlike Eridian life)
- Can survive in Venus-like and Threeworld-like atmospheres
- Breeds exponentially when given Astrophage to consume
- Different strains tolerate different temperatures; Taumoeba-82.5 works for both solar systems
- **Critical flaw**: Some strains can eat through xenonite — this nearly killed Rocky

### The Solution
- Seed Venus (near Sol) and Threeworld (near 40 Eridani) with Taumoeba
- Taumoeba eats the breeding Astrophage populations, reducing them to insignificance
- Grace sent Taumoeba to Earth via "beetles" (mini-probes)
- Rocky brought Taumoeba back to Erid in his fuel bays (22 million kg of it)

### As Food
- Rocky suggested Grace eat Taumoeba to survive on Erid
- It worked — Taumoeba provided calories (glucose) but not balanced nutrition
- Grace became malnourished (scurvy, beriberi) before Eridians synthesized vitamins

## 3. Xenonite

### What It Is
- Eridian super-material, made primarily from xenon (a noble gas on Earth)
- Rocky's ship, tools, and structures are made from it
- Multiple varieties exist — different xenonite compositions for different applications

### Properties
- Extremely hard (harder than diamond in some formulations)
- Not brittle — can absorb impacts
- Excellent thermal insulator (Grace can touch xenonite that has 210C air on the other side)
- Can be transparent or opaque depending on formulation
- Can be glued together with xenonite-based adhesive
- Can be shaped into flat panels (square airlock doors) or curved forms (tunnel, geodesic ball)
- Strong enough that flat panels work as pressure vessels (no need for curved shapes to distribute force)

### Vulnerability
- Certain Taumoeba strains can eat through xenonite
- This is the critical plot crisis — Rocky's xenonite hull is compromised by Taumoeba

### In the App
- Rocky's name for the material should always be "xenonite" (proper noun)
- Rocky takes pride in xenonite engineering: "My xenonite knowledge is beyond dispute"
- Material of choice for everything Rocky builds

## 4. The Blip-A (Rocky's Ship)

### Name Origin
- Grace named it "Blip-A" — the first blip on his radar

### Design
- Eridian-built interstellar ship
- Uses Astrophage fuel for spin drives (same principle as the Hail Mary)
- Hull made entirely of xenonite
- No computers — Eridians never invented them ("Machine that think. Eridians no have that.")
- All navigation done by manual calculation (Eridians are excellent at mental math)
- Robot arms on the hull for EVA work — controlled remotely from inside
- Square/hexagonal design elements (airlocks, panels) — Eridian engineering aesthetic

### Crew
- Original crew of 23 Eridians
- All died from radiation sickness except Rocky
- Rocky survived because his workshop was between Astrophage fuel tanks

### Internal Environment
- 29 Earth atmospheres pressure
- Ammonia atmosphere
- 210 degrees Celsius
- Pitch dark (Eridians don't need light)

## 5. The Hail Mary (Grace's Ship)

### Purpose
- Humanity's last-hope mission to Tau Ceti to find a solution to Astrophage
- Named "Hail Mary" — a desperate play

### Design
- Astrophage-powered spin drives
- Centrifuge for artificial gravity (crew compartment rotates)
- Lab, dormitory, control room, storage compartment
- Three-person crew (DuBois replaced by Grace at last minute)
- Coma system for the multi-year journey
- "Beetles" — four mini-probes that can be sent back to Earth with samples

### Crew
- Commander Yáo (died in coma)
- Olesya Ilyukhina (died in coma — left vodka stash behind)
- Ryland Grace (sole survivor — woke up with amnesia)

## 6. Eridian Engineering (Rocky's Approach)

### Philosophy
- Practical, hands-on, mechanical solutions
- No computers, no software — everything is analog/mechanical
- Rocky's tools: bandolier of hand tools, satchel of parts
- "Slower is safer. Less mistakes."

### Capabilities
- Built a life-support ball (geodesic xenonite sphere + AC unit) to survive in Grace's environment
- Uses Astrophage as air conditioning (absorbs heat above 96C)
- Built a mini-airlock ("squarelock") for exchanging objects
- Built tunnel modifications using hull robots
- Can repair/fabricate xenonite structures
- Can create Taumoeba life-support farms (automatic, years-long, under 1kg)

### Limitations
- No computers — cannot do software/simulation
- No understanding of radiation until crew started dying
- No concept of light/vision — Rocky is amazed by human sight
- Analog instruments with raised/tactile readouts

## 7. The Spin Drive

- Propulsion system used by both ships
- Astrophage is the fuel — releases stored energy as thrust
- Achieves near-light-speed travel
- Both Earth and Erid independently developed this technology from studying Astrophage

## 8. Petrova Wavelength

- The specific infrared wavelength at which Astrophage absorbs stellar energy
- Named after Dr. Petrova, who first characterized Astrophage
- Key to detecting and studying Astrophage populations around stars
- Grace built a "Petrovascope" — a detector tuned to this wavelength

## 9. The Beetles

- Four small, autonomous probes aboard the Hail Mary
- Designed to carry samples (including Taumoeba) back to Earth
- Grace sent them toward Earth before deciding to save Rocky
- They successfully delivered Taumoeba to Earth (confirmed when Sol returns to full luminance)

## 10. The Thrum (Eridian Collective Intelligence)

- A unique Eridian communication/decision-making process
- Many Eridians talk simultaneously — the group arrives at emergent conclusions
- "The thrum itself is much smarter than any Eridian in it"
- "Eridians can become ad-hoc neurons in a group mind"
- They come and go freely — voluntary, not compelled
- Used for major scientific/societal decisions
- Second-largest thrum ever held was to solve "how to keep Grace alive on Erid"
